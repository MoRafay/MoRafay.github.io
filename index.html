About Me

I am an M.S. student in Biomedical Engineering at Kyung Hee University, South Korea, under the supervision of Professor Ki Joo Pahk. My research focuses on advancing non-invasive therapeutic ultrasound, specifically optimizing pre-treatment algorithms for boiling histotripsy. I am developing high-precision focus prediction models using deep learning to enhance medical imaging and monitor acoustic cavitation.
