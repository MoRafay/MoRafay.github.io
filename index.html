About Me

I am an M.S. student in Biomedical Engineering at Kyung Hee University, advised by Prof. Ki Joo Pahk. My research focuses on advancing non-invasive therapeutic ultrasound, with a particular emphasis on optimizing pre-treatment algorithms for boiling histotripsy. I am currently developing high-precision focus prediction models to improve the targeting accuracy of therapeutic ultrasound. I am also interested in leveraging deep learning for medical imaging and in developing robust methods for real-time monitoring of acoustic cavitation.
