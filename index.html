About Me

I am a Master’s researcher at the Biomedical Ultrasound Engineering Lab at Kyung Hee University, working under the guidance of Prof. Ki Joo Pahk. My academic journey is centered on advancing therapeutic ultrasound, with a particular focus on optimizing pre-treatment algorithms for Boiling Histotripsy.

My current research activities involve implementing high-precision focus prediction models to bridge the gap between numerical simulations and experimental validation. By utilizing k-Wave simulations and Passive Cavitation Imaging (PCI), I strive to enhance the accuracy and safety of non-invasive surgical procedures.
