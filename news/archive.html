Research Journey

A chronological record of my academic activities and research milestones at KHU.

Nov 2025

Excellent Research Award

Contributed to the research presented at the 11th Korean Society for Therapeutic Ultrasound (KSTU) Conference and received the Excellent Research Award.

May 2025

Outstanding Research Poster Award

Participated in the 65th Korean Society of Medical & Biological Engineering (KSMBE) Conference and was awarded the Outstanding Research Poster Award.

Nov 2024

Outstanding Research Paper Award

Received the Outstanding Research Paper Award at the 64th KSMBE Conference for our work on therapeutic ultrasound.

Oct 2024

Seoul Tech Scholarship

Awarded the Seoul Tech Scholarship in recognition of academic excellence and research potential.

Sept 2024

Oral Presentation at ISTU 2024

Delivered an oral presentation on "AI model of the prediction of boiling histotripsy focus: A proof-of-concept study" at the 23rd ISTU in Taipei, Taiwan.

Sept 2024

Commenced Master's Program

Began my M.S. degree in Biomedical Engineering at Kyung Hee University, focusing on therapeutic ultrasound algorithms.

Aug 2024

B.S. in Biomedical Engineering

Successfully graduated from Kyung Hee University with a Bachelor’s degree in Biomedical Engineering.

Nov 2023

KSMBE Conference Presentation

Presented my work on "Detecting a skin layer using the Canny edge algorithm during HIFU treatment" at the 62nd KSMBE Conference.

Oct 2023

KSTU Annual Symposium

Presented research on cavitation energy analysis during boiling histotripsy and pressure-modulated shockwave histotripsy.

Nov 2022

Outstanding Research Paper Award

Received the Outstanding Research Paper Award as part of a team project at the KSMBE Conference.

Oct 2022

KSTU Poster Presentation

Presented a poster on violent acoustic cavitation produced by 20 kHz ultrasound at the 8th KSTU Conference.

May 2022

Joined BUEL as Undergraduate Researcher

Joined the Biomedical Ultrasound Engineering Lab (BUEL) at KHU to begin my research journey.
