Developing Robust Beamforming Algorithms for Real-Time PCI Analysis

Feb 1, 2026 By Muhammad Rafay Mahmood

As part of my ongoing research at the Biomedical Ultrasound Engineering Lab (KHU), I have been focusing on improving the accuracy of focus localization in non-homogeneous media. Passive Cavitation Imaging (PCI) has been a cornerstone of this effort.

During the recent analysis phase, our team identified a gap between computational modeling and experimental validation. To address this, I implemented a refined beamforming algorithm using MATLAB that significantly reduces noise in the cavitation monitoring process.

"The integration of high-precision focus prediction models with real-time PCI analysis is essential for the future of safe, non-invasive ultrasonic surgeries."

The next steps involve validating these results with the B-mode ultrasound scan data we acquired during the pig in-vivo experiments in late 2025. We expect these findings to contribute to our upcoming paper on Boiling Histotripsy focus prediction.
